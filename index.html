Field Manual

Navigation & Combat

  • Move & Fire: Mouse drag / Touch drag. Weapons auto-fire while moving.
  • Warp Dash: Right-Click / Double-Tap. Grants brief invincibility and destroys trails.
  • EMP Shockwave: Press [SPACE]. Clears all enemies and projectiles on screen. Charges via score.

Classes & Artifacts

  • ⚖️ Balanced: 3HP, Avg Speed
  • 🥷 Rogue: 2HP, High Speed
  • 🛡️ Tank: 5HP, Low Speed

Power-Ups

  • S Kinetic Shield
  • W Weapon Overcharge
  • + Hull Repair (+1 HP)
  • D Wingman Drones

Mission Structure

  • Upgrades: Every wave threshold triggers a permanent augmentation choice (Hull, Speed, Weapons).
  • Bosses: Extreme threats spawn periodically based on your score limit.
  • Co-Op Revives: Click the REVIVE button next to a dead teammate's name in the HUD to instantly revive them. Max 3 revives per pilot.
  • Spectator: When dead, press [SPACE] to switch views between surviving allies.
